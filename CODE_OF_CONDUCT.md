# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Treat contributors and users with respect. Constructive disagreement is welcome;
harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and sustained disruptive behavior are
not.

Examples of expected behavior include:

- giving specific, technical, and actionable feedback;
- respecting differing experience levels and viewpoints;
- accepting correction and taking responsibility for mistakes;
- keeping discussions focused on the project.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions, comments, issues, or other
participation that violates this standard. They may temporarily or permanently ban
participants for serious or repeated violations.

Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact options on their
GitHub profile. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.
